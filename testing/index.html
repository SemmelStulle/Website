Why does this exist?

There are a lot of inconsistencies within Minecraft itself.
Why is the Redstone Torch a normal Torch with 8 red pixels?
Why not have clean Stained Glass?
Do you think, Suspicious Sand or Gravel are too hard to see?
I try to spot and fix all these things without having to give up the vanilla design.

Might there be anorher pack that does the same things but better? Probably! But this is my personal take on it for my own Realm.
